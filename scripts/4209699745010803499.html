COVID-19: Sex Workers, Clients Arrested In Ondo For Flouting Government Lockdown Directive

The sex workers were apprehended over the weekend at a brothel in the Shasha area of Akure, the state capital.

27 Apr 2020 by SaharaReporters, New York

At least 18 commercial sex workers have been arrested in Ondo State for flouting the dusk to dawn curfew meant to prevent the spread of Coronavirus in the state. 

The sex workers were apprehended over the weekend at a brothel in the Shasha area of Akure, the state capital.
